Who We Are

Burnt-Forest Sub County Hospital is a premier healthcare facility located in Uasin Gishu County, providing comprehensive medical services to the community. Our goal is to offer affordable, high-quality healthcare to every patient.

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, compassionate healthcare that meets the highest standards of care for the local community and beyond.

Services We Offer

  • General Medical Services
  • Specialist Consultations
  • 24/7 Emergency Services
  • Maternity Care
  • Surgical Services
  • Laboratory Testing
  • Radiology and Imaging
  • Ambulance Services

Our Team

We have a team of qualified doctors, nurses, and medical staff who are committed to providing the best care possible. Our team is continuously trained to keep up with the latest advancements in medical technology and treatment.

Why Choose Us

  • State-of-the-art facilities
  • Affordable healthcare services
  • Dedicated and professional staff
  • Comprehensive care in one location
