Our Ambulance Services

We provide 24/7 ambulance services to handle all medical emergencies. Our ambulances are equipped with life-saving equipment and trained paramedics to ensure timely and safe transportation of patients.

How to Request an Ambulance

To request an ambulance, please call our emergency line at +254 711 987 654 and provide details of the emergency. We will dispatch the nearest available ambulance to your location.

Ambulance Rates

  • Within Burnt-Forest Town: KES 2,000
  • Outside Town (up to 50km): KES 5,000
  • Long Distance (above 50km): Contact us for pricing
